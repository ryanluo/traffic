Traffic Simulation

Adding Traffic

Now let's add some more rules:

  • Cars follow the three second rule. If a car is less than three seconds away from the car in front of it, it will deaccelerate such that it comes to a complete stop within 2 seconds.
  • If a car is not stuck behind the car in front of it and it is moving slower than it's maximum speed, it will accelerate at a constant 3 m/s until it reaches its maximum velocity.
  • I introduced a bit of randomness among maximum velocity (+/- 1 kph)
  • Cars are now color-coded by speed. Green is faster, red is slower.
  • To make things interesting, I introduce one car that has a far larger maximum speed than the others (road rage). This system will gradually settle into something of an equilibrium, where a choo-choo train forms behind the slowest car (traveling at its maximum velocity).

Positions and velocities are computed using simple Euler integration.
